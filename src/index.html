about

this project

This project was an assignment for ARTD 313 at UIUC, and was completed in April 2014. The assignment was to create a digital adaptation of a children's story, designed specifically for an iPad.

this designer

Kashev Dalmia is an engineer who took a Graphic Design class once at University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. He is probably out of his element.
